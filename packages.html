HOW TO PREPARE FOR YOUR PHOTO SESSION

When getting your photos taken, there are things you rely on your photographer to do well: posing, lighting, and composition. As a client and the subject, you should be able to show up and let the magic happen, right? There are several things you can do to prepare for your photo session and make it even better. I’ve built a comprehensive list of things you can do to make your session a tremendous success.

  1. Please leave your adorable pets and kids at home, unless it’s absolutely impossible or agreed with photographer (or your precious is a part of photo shoot). For the best result let yourself be free and relaxed, concentrated on yourself only while shooting.
  2. Pick out your clothes early. Once you know what you should wear, make sure to assemble your wardrobe a few days before your session. Get items laundered, ironed, and anything else needed so that you’re not rushing at the last minute. Another helpful tip is to figure out how you’ll change your clothes on location.
  3. Gather your props. If you are utilizing props for your portraits, make sure to assemble them a few days before the shoot.
  4. Please wash your hair at the day of shooting. You will not regret, I promise.
  5. If you have opportunity, invite a make-up artist. If you can do makeup and hair yourself, please bring makeup bag and matting facial tissues with you.
  6. It’s convenient to wear seamless beige underwear - it fits everything.
  7. Get on the same page as your photographer. To have a successful photoshoot, it’s a great idea to share photo concepts with your photographer, so you are going for the same result. Pinterest boards are my favorite way to communicate concepts.
  8. Please include bottle of water to you list.
  9. Be on time (better earlier)! Photographer might have tough schedule. If you are shooting outside, sun will not wait. Studios are rented hourly. You do not want to waste money.
